About Acurat

We believe tax review deserves better tools

CPA firms spend thousands of hours every season on manual review — checking the same lines, catching the same errors, chasing the same carryovers. We started Acurat because we believe agentic AI can fundamentally change how returns are reviewed — not by replacing CPAs, but by giving them an intelligent first pass they can trust.

Our platform is built by a team that understands both tax and AI deeply. Every agent is grounded in IRS publications and IRC sections. Every finding is traceable to a source. And every escalation is designed to respect the professional judgment that only a human reviewer can provide.

Accuracy First

Every finding backed by IRS publications. No hallucinated tax advice — ever.

CPA in Control

AI handles volume. Your team handles judgment. Confidence scoring keeps humans in the loop.

Domain Expertise

12 agents, each a specialist. From mortgage interest to passive activity rules.

Your Practice, Your Rules

40+ configurable skills. Enable what your firm needs, disable what it doesn't.
